faqs:
  - question: "What is Dialcash and what is it for?"
    answer: >
      Dialcash is a mobile application designed to help you keep a clear and simple
      track of your daily expenses and income. It allows you to record transactions,
      analyze your money, and improve your personal finances without complications.

  - question: "Is Dialcash a free application?"
    answer: >
      Yes. Dialcash is completely free and does not require subscriptions or hidden payments.
      All its main features are available from the first moment.

  - question: "Do I need an internet connection to use Dialcash?"
    answer: >
      No. Dialcash works offline. You can record your expenses and income
      without connection and continue using the app normally.

  - question: "Where is my financial data stored?"
    answer: >
      All your information is saved locally on your device. Dialcash does not send
      your data to external servers nor shares it with third parties.

  - question: "Is Dialcash secure?"
    answer: >
      Yes. By not relying on the cloud or online accounts, your data remains under your
      control. The information only exists on your phone.

  - question: "Can I lose my data if I change phones?"
    answer: >
      If you change devices without making a backup, the data will not transfer
      automatically. It is recommended to make periodic backups if the app allows it.

  - question: "Who is Dialcash designed for?"
    answer: >
      Dialcash is designed for anyone who wants better control of their money: students,
      freelancers, entrepreneurs, or any user looking to organize their personal finances.

  - question: "What type of expenses can I record?"
    answer: >
      You can record any type of expense or income, from daily purchases to
      recurring payments, without restrictions.

  - question: "Does Dialcash replace a banking app?"
    answer: >
      No. Dialcash is not a banking app and does not handle real transactions. It is a
      personal financial organization tool.

  - question: "Does the app have advertising?"
    answer: >
      No. Dialcash does not include ads that interfere with the user experience.

  - question: "On which platforms is Dialcash available?"
    answer: >
      Currently Dialcash is available as a mobile application. Availability
      may vary depending on the operating system and future updates.

  - question: "How do I start using Dialcash for the first time?"
    answer: >
      When you open the app, you can start recording your expenses immediately. It is not
      necessary to create an account or perform complex configurations.
